Nick Niles
is an American-born and NYC-educated graphic and motion designer living and working in Zürich, Switzerland. He is a Senior Interactive Designer at Atfront in Zürich.


His range of skills encompasses art direction, brand identity, graphic design, illustration, 2D and 3D modeling and animation, UI/UX design, HTML5/CSS/JS front-end development, cinematography and music composition. He holds an MFA in Film Directing from Columbia University in NYC. He has worked on teams both large and small and served as a one-stop-shop for startup companies shaping their visual image.

Recent clients include Unilever, Sony, Coca-Cola, Hilton Hotels and Thomson Reuters.
